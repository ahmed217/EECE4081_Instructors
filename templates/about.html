This an section project of the Class scheduling project for the Herff College of Engineering of the UofM. This program will be used to add instructors as well as their related information such as title and LinkedIn page, University profile, or personal website. It also will be used to manage instructors in the database with various functions like Delete and Update.
It was developed in Fall semester of 2020-21 by team Z-Blue, assignment by Dr.Faruk Ahmed.
